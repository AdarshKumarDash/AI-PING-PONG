AI PING PONG GAME







Created By - Adarsh Kumar Dash || Game Credit: AI Ping Pong

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight.

  2. Move yourself approximately 2-3 feet away from the laptop.

  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

  4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 2-3 feet away from laptop.

  5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your wrist.
